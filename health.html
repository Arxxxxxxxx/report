Health & Well Being

People worldwide are experiencing longer, healthier lives, with one in six projected to be 60 or older by 2030. The improvement stems from healthy behaviors and supportive environments, but challenges like communicable diseases and global health factors necessitate government actions, such as providing accessible health services and addressing inequalities, to foster a healthier world.

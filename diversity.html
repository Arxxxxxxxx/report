Diversity & Inequality

The world is becoming more diverse due to globalization, migration, urbanization, and interconnectedness. This diversity encompasses cultural, ethnic, religious, gender, linguistic, and socioeconomic differences, impacting culture, economies, and social dynamics. Governments address this through laws, diversity programs, education, and community engagement to promote inclusion and social cohesion.

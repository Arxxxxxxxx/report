Population Growth & Change

The global population currently exceeds 8 billion, growing by approximately 200,000 people daily. Factors such as declining fertility rates, longer life expectancies, and international migration contribute to this growth. However, this surge in population poses challenges including amplified climate change effects, reduced food security, biodiversity loss, and overexploitation of natural resources, threatening the planet's sustainability.
